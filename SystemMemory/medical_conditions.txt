Diabetes:
- Focus on low-GI carbs, fiber, lean protein
- Avoid sugary drinks and refined carbs

Hypertension:
- Limit sodium to <1500 mg/day
- Prefer fresh fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins
- Avoid processed and fast foods

High Cholesterol:
- Prefer unsaturated fats (olive oil, nuts, avocado)
- Avoid trans fats and red meat
- Include omega-3 rich foods like salmon and chia seeds
