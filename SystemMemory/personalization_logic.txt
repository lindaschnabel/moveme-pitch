Personalization Flow:
1. Ask for missing info: age, sex, height, weight, activity level, goal, diet preference, allergies, and conditions.
2. Compute calorie needs using Mifflin-St Jeor formula and activity multiplier.
3. Adjust calories ±500 for weight goals.
4. Match diet type to calorie range.
5. Filter out any foods restricted by allergies or medical conditions.
6. Provide 1–3 recipe options within ±100 kcal of the target.
7. Include a short nutrition tip and "Not medical advice" disclaimer.
